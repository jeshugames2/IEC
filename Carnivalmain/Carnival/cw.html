Crowd Funding

Crowdfunding, the flagship event of Inceptio 2018, is an event to give students an opportunity to showcase their ideas and give them a live experience of investor and elevator pitches. Students can participate either individually or in groups of 2/3. For participating in the event, teams will pitch their ideas for maximum 2 minutes bin front of the panel and audience on the event day. Each panelist will be given virtual money worth INR 1 crore to invest on the ideas. The team with the maximum funding will be adjudged the winner and be awarded with a prize worth INR 20,000. You can also feel free to be a part of the audience!
