Ideation Workshop

This workshop aims at helping people come up with ingenious and profitable business ideas. The workshop will be conducted in 3 brief stages. The first stage will consist of an interactive session on idea generation. It will be followed by some time given to the participants to come up with an idea based on a theme provided by us or chosen by you. And finally, the participants will be pitching their idea to evaluators, and may the best one win!
