Idea Validation WorkShop

Give your startup Idea the seal of approval! In the Idea Validation Workshop, you can present your idea to a test audience, with expert Dr. Kaustubh Dhargalkar, and receive his constructive criticism on it, to help launch it in the real world. A question and answer session with the test audience will help you find the gaps in your idea, and will prompt you to bridge them with practical solutions. Dr. Kaustubh Dhargalkar is the workshop’s guiding force. His words have the ability of making one lose track of time. He founded Potentials and Possibilities, an organisation to break down all barriers to realising a business’s, and an individual’s, full potential, a sentiment IEC has embodied. He has helped students transition smoothly into the corporate world, as the Associate Dean for Business Design and Innovation at Welingkar Institute of Management. He has also mentored the Center for Innovation and Enterprise at IIM, Ahmedabad, and the Economic Times Power of Ideas, always endeavouring to promote possibilities and a creative thought process. Validating a start-up idea is essential for the success of the venture, and what better way is there to to do just that, than the Idea Validation Workshop?
