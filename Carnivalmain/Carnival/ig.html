Idea Generation

This workshop aims at helping people come up with ingenious and profitable business ideas. The workshop will be conducted in 3 brief stages. The first stage will consist of an interactive session on idea generation. It will be followed by some time given to the participants to come up with an idea based on a theme provided by us or chosen by you. And finally, the participants will be pitching their idea to evaluators, and may the best one win! It will be conducted by Mr. Nikhil Karkera, a consultant at Oracle Financial Services. He has done his bachelors in Computer Engineering. He has been a Quality Assurance Analyst Intern at Housing.com and has been with Oracle for the past 4 years.
