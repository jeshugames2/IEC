About SVKM

Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal is a Public Charitable Trust registered under the Society’s Registration Act and Bombay Public Trust Act. From its humble beginnings in 1934, when it took over the Rashtriya Shala, a school established in 1921 in the wake of the National Movement, the Mandal today has grown into a big educational complex imparting high-level education to more than 35,000 students.

About NMIMS

NMIMS is one of the fastest growing and the top, private University in the country. It has undergone a tremendous transformation since its commencement. Today, it stands as a large imposing University with 9 specialized schools plus two off-Campus operations, with an intake of over 6000 students and over 430 full time faculty members. From its temporary location at Bhaidas Auditorium, NMIMS has moved to a large complex of over 40000 sq feet where it stands today as a landmark in Vile Parle, an affluent suburb of Mumbai.

About MPSTME

Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management & Engineering (MPSTME) is the important addition to NMIMS’ vision of ‘Transcending Horizons’. MPSTME leverages the capabilities inherent in the university, in both engineering and management domains to make this unique contribution possible. MPSTME is equipped with a world-class library, which has an impressive collection of books and journals. It is fast transforming itself into a digital library with e-models, e-catalogues and an online database-Online Public Access Catalog on the intranet, which helps students check the library’s collection of titles from across the world.

Innovation Cell

Innovation Cell was started with the aim of inculcating innovative thinking in the minds of young individuals. Students are encouraged to think beyond textbooks and discover their hidden interests through the course of events and discussions we conduct. All the resources required to build projects are facilitated by the Innovation Cell for the benefit of students.

Entrepreneurship Cell

With the core belief that Entrepreneurship is the key to India's development and growth, we work for bridging the gap between an idea and its execution. E-Cell has been succesful in creating an entrepreneurial environment in the college. We have succesfully conducted various guidance sessions and seminars in this domain, and provided support to startups from in house student entrepreneurs in variousstages of their work.
